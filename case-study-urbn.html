The Client

A Multi-Concept Catering Powerhouse

urbn catering is a multi-concept catering company serving over 5,000 people daily through corporate catering, events, and meal programs across multiple locations. With rapid growth outpacing their team's capacity, the owner was working 70+ hours a week and facing a choice: hire 3–4 additional staff members, or find a better way.

The Problem

Scaling Faster Than Operations Could Handle

The business was expanding into new markets, but every part of the operation was bottlenecked by manual work:

  • 100–150 weekly inquiries, each requiring a custom manual proposal
  • Disorganized event tracking and team communication
  • Invoicing bottlenecks with delayed Square payments
  • Manual sales tax calculations for every location
  • No client follow-up or repeat booking system
  • Limited visibility into per-event profitability
  • Expansion to LA and Silicon Valley seemed impossible without hiring
The Solution

Cynthia as the Operations Backbone

Cynthia was implemented as a full-stack AI employee, handling seven core operational areas:

1

Lead Management & Proposals

Monitors inquiries, extracts event details, generates custom proposals with e-signature capability, and auto-follows up.

2

Scheduling & Coordination

Integrates with calendars, auto-schedules events, and sends job sheets directly to kitchen and service teams.

3

Invoicing & Payments

Generates Square invoices same-day, tracks outstanding receivables, and runs automated follow-ups at 7, 14, and 30 days.

4

Sales Tax Automation

Automatically looks up location-based tax rates for every event, eliminating manual calculations.

5

Client Communication

Sends confirmation texts, thank-you messages, review requests, and identifies repeat booking opportunities.

6

Profitability Analysis

Calculates margins per event, identifies cost drivers, and delivers weekly P&L reports by event type.

7

Market Expansion

Replicates all workflows for new locations — enabling dual-market launches with zero additional hires.
