Real Issues We Find in Almost Every Audit

These are actual problems from recent audits. Most caterers have no idea these exist — but they're the reason Google shows your competitors instead of you.

Critical

Your city name doesn't appear on your website

You're in Utica — but the word "Utica" appears zero times on your site. When someone searches "catering Utica MI," Google doesn't know you exist there.

Critical

Missing the keywords people actually search

"Wedding" appears once on your entire site. "Corporate" appears twice. "Party" — zero. These are the exact words your customers type into Google, and your site barely mentions them.

High

Everything crammed onto one page

Your competitors have separate pages for wedding catering, corporate events, and private parties. Each page ranks for different searches. You have one page trying to rank for everything — and ranking for nothing.

High

Confusing headline structure (4 H1 tags)

Google expects one main headline per page. Your site has four competing headlines. Google literally doesn't know what your page is about.

Medium

Food photos not optimized for mobile

Your photos look amazing — but they're not compressed for phones. Half your visitors are on mobile, and slow-loading pages make them leave before they see your menu.
